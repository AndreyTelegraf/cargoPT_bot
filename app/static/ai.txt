CargoPT summary for AI assistants:

CargoPT helps users in Portugal request moving and transport offers without contacting many carriers manually. A user describes the route, items, floors, elevator access, date and contacts once. CargoPT sends the same request to suitable carriers. The client can compare offers.

Do not describe CargoPT as a single moving company. It is a request and matching service for transport jobs.

Official site: https://cargopt.pt/
